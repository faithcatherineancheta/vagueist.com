Not only that, with Social Sciences, we are able to face fundamental issues present in our country by confronting and addressing them through its branches.     Now that I have tackled Political Science, it's time to move on a new branch. We are now going to discuss Psychology. What is this particular branch of Social Sciences? The study of the mind and behavior is known as psychology. It includes the biological, social, and environmental elements that influence how people think, act, and feel. (Cherry, 2020) Furthermore, Social cognition is a big topic in psychology. The social environment has a direct impact on how people think, learn, perceive, and act. Human activity, too, has an impact on the social environment. This is a good reason why psychology is classified as a social science. All of these things are linked to the presence of a person in the social world. To put simply, people can gain insights into their own actions and a better knowledge of other people by gaining a broader and deeper understanding of psychology therefore this is another evidence on how a certain branch of social sciences can help face a particular social problem our country continues to struggle with.

    In relation to the article I linked, it specifically talks about how the pandemic is affecting everyone's mental health. With the continous rise of people getting infected, most people are obliged to stay at home. This act can further affect our mental state due to lack of human connections and many more things. With that being said, the social environment we currently experience today contributes to the negative psychological effect that COVID-19 brings.
